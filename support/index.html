Sand Life Support

Sand Life is a mobile app for controlling sand art devices over Bluetooth.

Need help? Contact wuqidashi0109@gmail.com.

Common Checks

  • Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your phone.
  • Keep the sand art device powered on and nearby.
  • If scanning does not find the device, stop and start scanning again.
  • If the device still does not appear, turn your phone's Bluetooth off and on, then scan again.
  • If the app asks for Bluetooth permission, grant the permission and return to the app.
  • If connection remains unstable, restart the sand art device and try again.
